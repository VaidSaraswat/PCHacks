About Us

We believe that everyone has a story to tell. Inspiring stories of overcoming struggle and set-backs. We at PCHacks hope to provide a community where those with disabilies can share their experiences and find those who can relate to their struggles.

Service

PCHacks provides an open forum where those with physical or mental disabilities can share their stories and find inspiration in others. Not only but PCHacks provides people with tonnes of amazing paralympic sports that could be of potential interest to some people.
